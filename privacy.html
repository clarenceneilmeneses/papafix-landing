Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Pre-launch notice. The PapaFix app has not yet launched. Today this website collects only the email address you choose to give us for the launch waitlist. The sections below describing in-app data (location, bookings, payments) explain what will apply once the app is released, so you know in advance what to expect.

1. Who we are

PapaFix ("PapaFix", "we", "us") is an early-stage on-demand home-repair platform based in Tanauan, Batangas, Philippines. We are the data controller responsible for the personal information described in this policy. You can reach us any time at clarenceneilpamplona@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

2.1 From this website, right now

  • Email address — only if you submit it to the launch waitlist. Nothing else on this site asks for personal information.
  • Standard server logs — our hosting provider records requests (IP address, browser type, page requested, timestamp) as part of normal operation and security.

This site does not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking pixels. Waitlist submissions are delivered to our team inbox through the form-relay service Web3Forms, which processes your email address solely to pass it on to us.

2.2 From the app, once it launches

  • Account details — name, mobile number, email address.
  • Service address — where the repair job takes place.
  • Location data — for customers, to match you with nearby technicians. For technicians, live location while you are online and on an active job, so the customer can track arrival. Technicians' location is not collected while offline.
  • Booking records — service requested, job notes, photos you attach, price estimates, job status and completion history.
  • Payment information — handled by our payment provider. We receive confirmation of payment and do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Technician verification records — government ID and proof of trade, collected during in-person screening to verify identity and qualifications.
  • Device and diagnostic data — device model, OS version, and crash logs, to keep the app working.

3. How we use your information

  • To notify you when PapaFix launches in your city (waitlist emails only — we will not add you to unrelated marketing).
  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match a booking with a suitable nearby technician and let you track them to your door.
  • To process payments and issue receipts.
  • To verify technician identity and qualifications before they take jobs.
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes about a job.
  • To keep the platform safe, prevent fraud and abuse, and meet our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers.

4. Who we share it with

We share personal information only where it is needed to run the service:

  • Between customer and technician — when a booking is confirmed, the assigned technician receives your first name, service address, and contact number. You receive the technician's name, photo, and rating. Neither party sees anything beyond what the job needs.
  • Service providers — hosting, payment processing, SMS/email delivery, and crash reporting, each bound to handle data only on our instructions.
  • Legal and safety — where required by Philippine law, a valid legal request, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.
  • Business transfer — if PapaFix is ever acquired or merged, personal information may transfer as part of that transaction. We will notify you first.

5. How long we keep it

Waitlist emails are kept until launch, or until you ask us to remove yours — whichever comes first. Account and booking records are kept for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterwards where we need them to resolve disputes, meet tax and accounting obligations, or enforce our terms. Verification records for technicians are kept for as long as they are active on the platform.

6. Your rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), you have the right to be informed about how your data is used, and to access, correct, object to the processing of, or request erasure or blocking of your personal information. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format, and lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission.

To exercise any of these rights — including removing yourself from the waitlist — email clarenceneilpamplona@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

This site is served over HTTPS. We apply reasonable organisational and technical measures to protect personal information, and limit access to those who need it to do their job. No system is perfectly secure, but if a breach ever affects your personal information, we will notify you and the National Privacy Commission as the law requires.

8. Children

PapaFix is not directed at children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has given us their information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy as the product develops — most notably when the app launches. When we make material changes we will revise the "last updated" date above and, where the change significantly affects you, notify you directly.
